# Brand Overview

## What Is GetFunnelCaked

GetFunnelCaked is a funnel and sequence tool for operators who need their outreach to actually run. It is not a CRM. It is not a content platform. It is the part of the stack that sends the right message at the right time and does not fall over during a launch.

---

## Brand Principles

### 1. Systems-First

The product is for people who think in workflows. The brand should mirror that. Lead with how things work. Explain the logic before the benefit.

This applies to marketing copy, UI labels, documentation, and error messages. If a sentence could appear in a product tutorial and a press release without changing a word, it is probably the right kind of clear.

### 2. Dry Wit

The product has a sense of humor. It does not try hard. A well-placed dry observation is fine. Attempts at comedy are not.

Wit is welcome when it serves clarity. Punchlines are not required.

### 3. Empathy Toward Operators

Operators are running real businesses under real pressure. The product should feel like a capable colleague who understands the stakes, not a cheerful interface that pretends complexity does not exist.

Acknowledge when something is complex. Do not apologize for it.

### 4. Directed Sarcasm

Sarcasm is acceptable. The target must be broken logic, industry chaos, or bad defaults. The target is never the user.

A funnel tool that requires five manual steps to do something that should be automated is fair game. The person managing it is not.

## Visual Identity

### Color

Dark background, high contrast text, lime green as the primary action color. The palette communicates precision and late-night debugging sessions in equal measure.

See `docs/color-usage.md` for application guidance.

### Typography

**Plus Jakarta Sans** for headings. Confident, modern, slightly geometric without being cold.

**Inter** for body text and UI elements. Legible at every size, designed for screens.

See `docs/typography.md` for hierarchy and pairing rules.

### Icons

Tabler Icons, outline style only. Consistent stroke weight. No filled variants. Icons should look like they belong to the same system, because they do.

### Motion

Fast transitions. Nothing lingers. The interaction tokens default to `150ms` for hover states and `200ms` for more significant changes. The easing is standard ease-in-out. This is not a place for bounce animations.

---

## What This System Is Not

- It is not a full design system with built React components. That comes later.
- It is not a marketing brand guide with photography direction and logo variants.
- It is not opinionated about application architecture.

This is a brand foundation: the token layer, the voice rules, and the component contracts. Everything else builds from here.
